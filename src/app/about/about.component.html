Who is he?

Hi there! I’m Tyler Guice - connoisseur of kaiju, pursuer of the paranormal, and learner of languages. Welcome to my humble abode among the cyberspace frontier.

Professionally, I’m a web developer with a passion for design and a proclivity for enhancing user experiences. I’ve been told that I'm creative enough to think outside the box to create aesthetic designs and experiences, yet technical enough to translate those ideas into a functional product.

In my spare time, you’ll find me meticulously organizing my Godzilla film collection, throwing on a Spanish language vinyl record, leafing through an art book, baking some exorbitantly sugary pastry, or picking up a game of tennis or disc golf.
